#### Decentralized Science ([DeSci](desci.md)) Hypotheses

1. **Big-cap corporation** [valuations](../CRYPTO/CRYPTO_ECONOMICS/valuations.md) have surpassed [trillions of dollars](../CRYPTO/CRYPTO_ECONOMICS/trillion_dollars.md). If [cryptocurrencies](../CRYPTO/CRYPTOCURRENCIES.MD) and [tokens](../CRYPTO/tokens.md) are accepted by [structural economic institutions](broken-reference) such as [pension funds](pension_funds.md) and [sovereign wealth funds](broken-reference), [DeSci organizations](non_human_intelligences.md) stand to realize the same [valuations](../CRYPTO/CRYPTO_ECONOMICS/valuations.md). However, current early-stage [DeSci](desci.md) organizations must “[position](../POSITIONING_STRATEGIES.md)” ([corporate, legal structural compatibility](ipcc.md)) themselves now.
2. [Medical and health sciences](../philosophy/consciousness.md) require billions of [patient](../strategy/economic_threshold.md) participants in large-scale [digital therapeutic](../DIGITAL_THERAPEUTICS.md) studies (unprecedented but “[required](broken-reference)”). These are only feasible via [DeSci](desci.md) approaches that entail [open-source coordination](../space/nasa.md) and [efficiency](phi4.md).
3. To facilitate accelerated progress in [science](broken-reference), a global, universal [citation chain](../AI/citation_chain.md) of [open-source science resources](../OPEN_SOURCE_SCIENCE_RESOURCES.md) ([science's counterpart to open educational resources](../space/disc_shaped_ufo.md) or [OERs](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Open_educational_resources)) is required.
